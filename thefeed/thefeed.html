Chapter 1:

Waking Up Is Hard...

When I have to wake up for my 10:30 ballet class, it is vital that I set at least three or four alarms. I wake up to an alarm of my favorite K-Pop idol singing a wake up song. To help my brain wake up, I sit up and look around my room to find ten small things that I am thankful for.

Chapter 2:

How Will SF Treat Us Today?

Because the sunshine in San Francisco is decieving, I check the weather to know how many layers I'll need to wear. No matter what the weather is, I find that I'm always cold in this city.

Chapter 3:

Will My Screen Time Increase if my Homescreen is Cute?

Today I reorganized my homescreen so that it's in my favorite color scheme and has widgets that reflect my favorite themes and are satisfying to look at.

Chapter 4:

Morning News Hour

In the morning, I listen to a news podcast called The Daily while I get dressed and do my makeup. Listening to the news makes me feel more conscious about what is happening in the world and I find that I feel less afraid when I understand more about the issues we are facing.

Chapter 5:

Coffee And Classwork

One of my favorite coffee shops is Four Barrell Coffee in the Mission. They have small tables and declicious, not-too-sweet hot chocolate. I love going to a coffee shop when it is rainy or when I need to do homework.

Chapter 6:

Doing Weekly Homework

I love making lists of all of the homework I have due by the end of the week and deleting each one as I go so that I can feel proud of what I have accomplished. Organizing my to-dos into lists helps prevent me from getting overwhelmed and scattered.

Chapter 7:

My Favorite Game

When I have downtime, I love opening Subway Surfers and playing a game or two. I am obsessed with the new cities and worlds that are costantly created for the game and how it looks a little different every time I open it. The visuals are actually what first got me really excited about graphic design!

Chapter 8:

Sisters are a Girl's Best Friend

My sisters and I are very close. We call or text every day to update each other and talking with them is an integral part of my life!

Chapter 9:

City Dance

When I have the time, I like to bus down to City Dance outside of the Castro to take hip hop classes. Although it's a bit of a trek, it is a welcoming community and a great place to improve my dancing.

Chapter 10:

Written In The Stars

Time to check my Costar Astrology app to see what my horoscope is for the day.I enjoy taking note of the specifics about how my energy is behaving today.

Chapter 11:

Pin It!

One of the best apps out there... Pinterest! I love looking at art inspiration, aesthetic food, and fun nails to save to my many boards. Like Spotify, Pinterest feels very personal and isn't as addictive as other social apps.

Chapter 12:

Read a New Book!

One of my favorite bookstores in the city is Dog Eared Books in the Mission. Although it takes a little while to bus there, it is worth it to go browse their poetry collection. If you can't tell by now, I am very dependant on Google Maps to navigate public transport and get me around SF.

Chapter 13:

On the Docket...

When I'm looking for fun events to put on my calendar for the month ahead, I will peruse EvenBrite. They always have good suggestions for free or cheap happenings in the bay area. I especially keep my eye out for music and art events.

Chapter 14:

The Voice of the People

Any question I have during the day? Straight to Reddit. My favorite unreliable source of information, Reddit is the voice of the public and provides many different opinions.

Chapter 15:

Happy Reading!

Before I read a book, such as the one featured above that I just started, I look on Goodreads to hear the general concensus on how well liked it is and if I am the reader it is suited for.

Chapter 16:

Third Space

My friends live off campus, a perfect 15-minute busride from campus. They gave me a key so that I can come visit whenever I want. It is my third space!
